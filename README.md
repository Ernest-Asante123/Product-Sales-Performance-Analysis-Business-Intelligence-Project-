# Product-Sales-Performance-Analysis-Business-Intelligence-Project-
Delivered a reliable and interactive reporting solution that improved visibility into product performance, enabling stakeholders to identify revenue drivers and make informed decisions to optimize sales strategies.
Tools: Excel (Pivot Tables, Dashboards, Data Visualization).

Business Problem:
The company lacked a centralized system to monitor sales performance across regions, dealers, and product categories, making it difficult to identify top-performing areas and optimize revenue strategies.

Objective:
To develop an interactive dashboard that provides clear insights into revenue trends, regional performance, and dealer contributions to support data-driven decision-making.

Key Contributions:
•	Designed and developed an interactive Excel dashboard to track revenue, sales volume, and regional performance. 
•	Analysed top-performing car brands contributing to overall revenue growth.
•	Identified high and low-performing regions to support strategic planning. 
•	Evaluated dealer performance distribution to highlight key business partners. 
•	Created dynamic filters (Year, Month) to enable real-time analysis.

Key Insights:
•	Ford and Chevrolet were identified as the top revenue-generating brands.
•	Certain regions showed declining revenue trends, indicating potential market challenges.
•	Sales distribution across dealers revealed uneven performance, suggesting opportunities for optimization.

Business Impact:
The dashboard enables management to quickly identify revenue drivers, monitor regional performance, and make informed decisions to improve sales strategy and resource allocation.
